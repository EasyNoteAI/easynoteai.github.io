About Us

Our mission:

To combine AI and music creation, allows music to serve any artist who wishes to create.


Do you believe that AI can create music? We firmly believe in this.

Using artificial intelligence technology, we have made quite a progress in the field of music composition, arrangement and mixing, and are moving towards the vision of "anytime, anywhere, music for anyone".


EasyNote wants to be the best assistant for artists in their creation. Some lyricists hope that there is a wonderful music worthy of their words, or some composers hope that there is a perfect arrangement worthy of their melody. We can all help.
